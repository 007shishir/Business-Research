Instruction

The book followed:

name:  Business Research Methods (12th Edition)

by Donald R. Cooper  and Pamela S. Schindler

There are two  main parts for this app. Those are as follows:

  1. Theory
  2. Practice and Exam

In the theory part, you will get a summary of each chapter. [I highly recommend reading the book first and then practice in the APP.]

There are two parts under Practice and Exam . They are:

  1. Memorize
  2. MCQ

A. Memorize  will help you memorize the basic definition and details through mnemonic. The steps are as follows:

  1. First select the card you would like to practice
  2. Second a question will appear in the question box
  3. Third, to see the answer, click on "Tap to see the answer"
  4. you will see the answer and explanation/mnemonic.
  5. If you know the answer, click "I knew this"
  6. If you do not know the answer, click "I didn't know this"
  7. Next question will appear on the screen.

⇒ Keep practicing as long as you are not master in that card

Note:

  1. Each question has a unique ID. (e.g., memorize_10000_q1)-q1 refers to the serial number of a question.
  2. The level of you progress is shown in the box below the ID.
  3. Name of the chapter will appear in the top left corner
  4. You will see the status of your progress for each question individually here:
    [Total level three: Primary; Learning; Master]
  5. Usually there are 40 Questions in each card. This portion will show summary of you progress for each question.
  6. If you find any error in the question or explanation, try to refresh the question by clicking on this
  7. If it does not work, send us an email on eee.videotutorial@gmail.com  mentioning the ID of that question.
    [The process of sending Email will be discussed later]

B. MCQ will help you to assess your level. The steps are as follows:

  1. First Click on the card you would like to practice.
  2. Second, read the question carefully and choose the answer. You are allowed to choose multiple options.
  3. Third, Click on SUBMIT button.
  4. In the explanation box, there will be an explanation/extra information regarding that question.
  5. Click NEXT to see the next question.

Keep practicing as long as you are not master in that card
